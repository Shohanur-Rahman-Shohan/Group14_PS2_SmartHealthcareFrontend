☁️ Backup & Recovery

Backup & Recovery ensures that all system data remains safe, recoverable, and protected against loss due to hardware failure, cyberattacks, or human error. By maintaining a robust and automated backup schedule, the IT team secures business continuity and data integrity even in critical situations.

Regular backups, off-site storage, and verification checks guarantee the highest level of system resilience. In case of data corruption or accidental deletion, rapid recovery procedures restore essential information swiftly, minimizing downtime and operational risks. This strategy keeps the organization’s infrastructure secure, recoverable, and always ready for the unexpected.
